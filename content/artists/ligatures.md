Born from ex-members of Pittsburgh 1990s hardcore outfits Pressgang and Crucial Unit, Ligatures are a D.C.-based post-hardcore band blending classic D.C. hardcore aggression, Chicago noise rock rhythmic dissonance, UK post-punk experimental dinginess, and outsider garage rock.

In 2023 Ligatures played their first shows in DC and Baltimore, blowing fuses and breaking equipment with disturbing frequency. Their debut LP *A23a* — named after the world's largest iceberg which recently separated from Antarctica — was recorded in Takoma Park, MD with Dan Godwin. Their follow-up *Agency* continues in the same vein of loud, fast, and artsy.
