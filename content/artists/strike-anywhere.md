Strike Anywhere are a Richmond, Virginia melodic hardcore band known for politically charged music and relentless touring. Their *Underground Europe 2001* EP on Scene Police was a Genoa benefit release, documenting the anti-G8 protests of 2001 and the band's solidarity with the movement.
